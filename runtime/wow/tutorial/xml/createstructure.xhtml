Create Thoretical/Conceptual Structure

Creating a structure is more of a pencil and paper thing and doesn't involve much of a use of a computer. Creating a structure may involve many steps like

  • Deciding on the information content of the application.
  • Dividing the information in your application into appropriate textual and graphical structures.
  • Sectioning and Sub-sectioning of the included content to create pages etc.
  • Deciding upon the hierarchy of the various pages.
  • Following a systematic naming guideline and assign resources to pages accordingly.

Our application will use the following strucuture (which more or less resembles the structure of a book )

  1. Welcome Page
  2. History of HTML
  3. Basic Constructs of HTML
    • Document Tags
    • Lists
    • Text Structure Tags
  4. Advanced Consructs of HTML
    • Special Tags
    • Anchors
  5. Final Words

The hierarchy of an application can be decided upon by various scientific structures or schemes. You can a choose a structure of your choice.
We present the hierarchy of our example using a tree structure, where the various nodes of the tree represent the various concepts or pages of our application.
